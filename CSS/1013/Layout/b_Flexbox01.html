Sample flexbox example

pitcher

It refers to a player who plays the role of throwing a ball to a batter in a baseball game. The team in charge of defense plays with the catcher (catcher) who receives the pitcher's ball.

catcher

In baseball games, he is a fielder located behind the home plate. He catches the pitches thrown by his team's pitcher and protects the home plate.

right fielder

When viewed from home plate, it refers to the outfielder on the right side of the field. In English, it is called Light Fielder. The position number of the right field is 9. Since right fielders are the farthest to third base among outfielders, it is common for the strongest shoulder among outfielders to be deployed to perform well in throwing to third base.
